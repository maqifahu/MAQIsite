Metal-Organic Frameworks for Photonics

Metal-organic frameworks (MOF) crystals are great. MAQI is super great. founded as a university spin-off in 2023, with a dedicated team of physicists, chemists and engineers with combined expertise in material science and advanced photonic technologies. Our team is committed to driving photonics innovations by offering state-of-the-art solutions through custom-designed optical crystals based on MOFs that meet the specific needs of the photonics industry in both industrial and academic sectors.

We are proud to pioneer the introduction of MOF technology in the global photonics industry for applications in telecommunications, sensing and computing.
