MOF Optical Crystals for Photonics

Metal-organic frameworks (MOF) are chemical structures that combine metal nodes with organic linkers into stable single crystals. The robust MOF chemistry offers unprecedented versatility of crystal assembly, allowing for the customization of MOF devices for applications in various domains ranging from gas purification to quantum optics.

The uniqueness of MOF crystals as nonlinear optical materials lies in their high chemical and thermal stability, resistance to optical damage, and the modularity of their optical properties. These characteristics, combined with a low-cost and energy-efficient manufacturing process, position MOFs as ideal materials to revolutionize the photonics industry.

Our company is dedicated to the mission of accelerating photonics innovations by offering state-of-the-art solutions through custom-designed optical crystals based on MOFs that meet the specific needs of the photonics industry in industrial, defense and academic sectors.
